What is the experience of the instructor(s) delivering my course? We only employ trainers who have plenty of real-world project management (and/or PRINCE2) experience. By doing so, all of our trainers are able to bring their experience into the classroom which helps you learn more effectively. In addition all of our PRINCE2 trainers have been rigorously accredited by the British Computer Society (one of the PRINCE2 accreditation bodies) to ensure that they have the appropriate PRINCE2 and project management experience.
